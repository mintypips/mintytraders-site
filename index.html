Frequently Asked Questions

The most common questions we get from new clients.

How does LIZA actually trade my account?
You connect your funded account (Apex, Tradovate, Rithmic, MT4/MT5, NinjaTrader, or TradeLocker) and LIZA executes trades automatically based on its proprietary signal engine. You retain full control of your account at all times — pause, stop, or close at any moment from your dashboard.
Will LIZA blow my funded account?
LIZA respects your prop firm's drawdown rules automatically. Every trade has a hard stop loss, daily loss limits are enforced, and the system shuts down if it approaches your account's max drawdown. No system can guarantee profits, but risk management is built into every trade.
Which prop firms are supported?
We support Apex, Topstep, Tradovate, Rithmic-based firms, MT4/MT5 brokers, NinjaTrader, and TradeLocker. If you're not sure whether your firm works, contact us and we'll confirm before you sign up.
What markets does LIZA trade?
LIZA actively trades the major US index futures: ES (S&P 500), NQ (Nasdaq 100), and YM (Dow). Starter accounts get ES only; Pro accounts get all three. Enterprise clients can request additional markets.
How fast can I get started?
Most clients are live within 24 hours of signup. The flow is: pay → onboarding call → connect your account → LIZA starts trading on the next session.
Do I need trading experience?
No trading experience required to use LIZA — the system handles execution. You do need a funded account already in place. If you don't have one yet, we can point you toward reputable prop firms during onboarding.
Is there a refund policy?
Setup fees cover real engineering and onboarding work, so they're non-refundable once your account is connected and live. The monthly Pro fee can be cancelled at any time. Reach out to our team if you have questions before signing up.

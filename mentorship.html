FAQ

The questions everyone asks before booking the call.

Do I need to know how to code?
No. You need to be willing to run commands and use an AI assistant. Claude (via OpenClaw) writes 95% of the code. I teach you how to direct it. If you can use a phone, you can do this.
Do I need a big Instagram following to find clients?
No. My first 5 clients came from under 3,000 followers. The playbook works on cold DMs and warm circles. Following helps, but it's not required — execution is.
What if I don't sign a single client?
If you complete the 4 weeks and don't sign a paying client, I keep working with you 1-on-1 until you do. That's the deal. The only way this fails is if you stop showing up.
How is this different from a trading course?
A trading course teaches you to trade your own account. This teaches you to build a service that runs itself and brings in MRR. You'll do both, but the goal is the business, not just your P&L.
How much capital do I need to start trading my own account?
You don't need a personal trading account to run a signal service. Many of my graduates use prop firm funded accounts ($150-$300 to start) to run their own signals while clients copy trade. We'll cover all of that in Week 3.
Will you give me your exact strategy?
I'll give you the architecture and the bot template. You'll develop your own strategy in Week 2 using my framework. If you ran my exact strategy on your clients, you'd just be a clone — that's not the goal.
How many people are in each cohort?
Max 5 per cohort. This is 1-on-1, not a group course. I cap it because I'm running my own service too — I can only give a few people the attention this requires.
How do I apply?
Click "Apply for the Mentorship" below. The application takes 5 minutes. If we're a fit, I'll book a 20-minute call to make sure we both want to work together. No high-pressure pitch — I'd rather lose a fee than take the wrong fit.
What about refunds?
Full refund within 7 days of starting if you decide it's not for you and have completed all Week 1 work. After Day 7, no refunds — but the outcome guarantee kicks in (I keep working until you sign a client).
